Prisoner Skills

We believe that there are a lot of prisoners out there who deserve second chances. Unfortunately, there is a huge societal stigma that has been cast onto all prisoners, making second chances seem impossible. We are here to break that stigma and to create mutually beneficial second chances along the way. So let us get you in touch with your new employee today!

What We Do

We seek to help forward-thinking inmates who are motivated to better themselves and prepare for their post-prison futures. The unemployment rate is tragically high for former inmates, so our goal is to give them a leg up. At the same time we give them the opportunity to develop and improve their skills while making some money along the way.

Did You know?

We don't always think about it, but some people out there are constantly struggling to provide for their families. These people, who have the best of intentions, sometimes, resort to "illegal means" just to get by and end up getting locked up. This is what we call, poverty related crime.

Why We Care!

We want to do our part to embetter society as a whole. Every person has the capacity to contribute to, to negatively effect and to do nothing for our communities. Our program enables prisoners to be productive members of society while they are locked up and increases the likliehood that they will continue to do so afterwards as well.
